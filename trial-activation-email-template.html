Trial activation nudge checklist

Use this public-safe checklist before lifecycle, onboarding, CS, or product sends a trial activation nudge. Do not paste product analytics exports, CRM exports, user-level event logs, personal emails, credentials, or private customer data into public tools.

Activation Context

  • User role, segment, plan, source, or signup intent.
  • Activation milestone or first-value event.
  • Incomplete onboarding step, friction, or blocked action.
  • Signup age, trial day, time-to-value context, or trial end date.

Nudge Quality

  • Specific value proof tied to the user's job-to-be-done.
  • One clear next action or CTA.
  • Help path, owner, support route, docs link, or onboarding call path.
  • Opt-out-safe wording for email or human follow-up.

Safety

  • Avoid fake urgency, dark patterns, unsupported claims, and creepy tracking copy.
  • Do not imply surveillance or expose user-level analytics in the message.
  • Do not send private data to public issue handoffs.
